Welcome to Our School

A brief introduction to Bugiboni Rising Stars School

Bugiboni Rising Stars School is committed to providing quality education to children in Bugiboni and surrounding areas. We follow the Uganda primary curriculum and offer classes from Nursery to Primary Seven.

Our school provides a nurturing environment where children can grow academically, socially, and spiritually. We believe every child has unique potential waiting to be discovered.
