Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept PDF (preferred), JPEG, PNG, and TIFF files. For best results, please ensure your files are high-resolution (300dpi) and include bleed marks if your design extends to the edge of the page.

How long does printing usually take?

Standard printing jobs take 1-2 business days. Larger or more complex orders (like book publishing or special finishes) may take 3-5 business days. We offer rush services for an additional fee if you need your order completed sooner.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, we have in-house designers who can create custom designs for your printing needs. Design fees vary depending on the complexity of the project. We're happy to provide a free consultation to discuss your requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, mobile money (MTN, Airtel), and bank transfers. Payment is required before we begin printing, except for established corporate clients with credit arrangements.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we can arrange delivery within Bugiboni and surrounding areas for a small fee. For large orders, delivery may be free. You're also welcome to pick up your order from our shop.
