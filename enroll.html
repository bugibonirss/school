Required Documents

After submitting this form, you will need to provide the following documents to complete enrollment:

  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Passport photos (4 copies)
  • Previous school report cards (if applicable)
  • Transfer letter (for students coming from other schools)
  • Immunization records

You may bring these documents when you come for the assessment or send them via email to admissions@risingstarsbugiboni.com
