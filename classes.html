Our Classes

Quality education for all levels

Nursery Section

Baby Class

For children aged 3 years. Our baby class focuses on developing basic social skills, language development, and early learning concepts through play-based activities.

Teacher: Mrs. Sarah Nalwoga

Class Size: 20 students maximum

Middle Class

For children aged 4 years. Building on foundational skills with more structured activities, early literacy and numeracy, and creative expression.

Teacher: Mr. Robert Kato

Class Size: 22 students maximum

Top Class

For children aged 5 years. Preparing children for primary school with more advanced learning activities, basic reading and writing skills, and simple mathematics.

Teacher: Mrs. Grace Nakato

Class Size: 25 students maximum

Primary Section

Primary One (P.1)

Introduction to formal education with focus on literacy, numeracy, and basic science. Developing good study habits and social skills.

Teacher: Mr. James Ssebaggala

Class Size: 30 students maximum

Primary Two (P.2)

Building on P.1 foundation with more complex concepts in all subjects. Emphasis on reading fluency and problem-solving skills.

Teacher: Mrs. Agnes Namukasa

Class Size: 30 students maximum

Primary Three (P.3)

Developing independent learning skills. Introduction to more abstract concepts in mathematics and science.

Teacher: Mr. David Muwonge

Class Size: 32 students maximum

Primary Four (P.4)

Consolidation of basic skills. Introduction to more complex problem-solving and critical thinking exercises.

Teacher: Mrs. Juliet Nansubuga

Class Size: 32 students maximum

Primary Five (P.5)

Preparation for upper primary. More advanced curriculum with emphasis on application of knowledge.

Teacher: Mr. Paul Wasswa

Class Size: 35 students maximum

Primary Six (P.6)

Focus on PLE preparation. Developing examination skills and time management.

Teacher: Mrs. Rose Nalule

Class Size: 35 students maximum

Primary Seven (P.7)

Final year of primary education. Intensive PLE preparation with mock examinations and revision programs.

Teacher: Mr. Henry Kigozi

Class Size: 35 students maximum
