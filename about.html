About Our School

Our history, mission and values

Our History

Bugiboni Rising Stars School was founded in 2010 with the vision of providing quality education to children in Bugiboni and surrounding areas. What started as a small nursery school has grown into a full-fledged primary school offering education from Nursery to Primary Seven.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures the intellectual, physical, social and moral development of our pupils in a conducive learning environment.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in primary education, producing well-rounded individuals who can contribute positively to society.

Our Values

  • Excellence in education
  • Integrity and honesty
  • Respect for all
  • Discipline and hard work
  • Innovation and creativity
