Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school

General Questions

What are the school hours?

The school operates from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM Monday to Friday. Nursery section ends at 1:00 PM. There are extracurricular activities on some afternoons.

Does the school provide meals?

Yes, we provide lunch for all students. There is also a morning tea break. Parents can choose to pay for the meals termly or annually.

What security measures are in place?

We have 24/7 security guards, controlled access to the school premises, and strict pickup policies to ensure student safety.

Admissions

What is the admission process?

Our admission process includes:

  1. Submission of application form
  2. Assessment for appropriate class placement
  3. Interview with parents/guardians
  4. Payment of registration fee
  5. Submission of required documents

What documents are required for admission?

You will need:

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school report cards (if applicable)
  • Passport photos (4 copies)
  • Immunization records
  • Transfer letter (for students coming from other schools)

Is there an entrance exam?

For Nursery admissions, there is no exam but an informal assessment. For Primary classes, we conduct basic competency tests in English and Mathematics to determine appropriate placement.

Fees and Payments

When are fees due?

Fees are due at the beginning of each term. There is a two-week grace period after which a late fee may be applied.

Are there any discounts for siblings?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount on tuition fees for the second child and 15% for the third and subsequent children from the same family.

What happens if we withdraw our child during the term?

If a student withdraws after the term begins, fees are refunded on a pro-rata basis, minus a 20% administrative charge. No refunds are given after the mid-term break.

Academics

What curriculum does the school follow?

We follow the Uganda National Primary School Curriculum as prescribed by the National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC).

How often are progress reports given?

We provide formal reports at the end of each term. Additionally, parents receive mid-term progress updates and can request meetings with teachers at any time.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer a variety of activities including:

  • Sports (football, netball, athletics)
  • Music, dance and drama
  • Scouting
  • Debate and public speaking
  • Art and craft clubs
